Privacy Policy for Funpath

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Funpath ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you choose to submit a bug report through the in-app bug report feature, the following information is collected and sent to us:

  • Bug category and description (as entered by you)
  • Device model and operating system
  • Screen resolution
  • Graphics hardware name
  • Device memory (RAM)
  • Current game level and scene
  • App version

This information is submitted only when you actively choose to send a bug report. It is not collected automatically.

Information Collected Automatically

To provide advertising and to understand and improve the game, the app and the third-party services it uses (see below) may automatically collect:

  • A device advertising identifier (Advertising ID) and/or app instance identifiers
  • IP address and approximate (coarse) location derived from it
  • Device and operating system information
  • In-app events and usage data (for example, levels started or completed, features used, ads shown)

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or postal address
  • Precise (GPS) location
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Browsing history
  • Your payment card or financial details (payments are processed by Google Play; see below)

How We Use Information

The information described above is used to:

  • Display advertisements in the free version of the game
  • Measure how the game is used so we can fix issues and improve it
  • Identify and fix bugs (bug report data)

Advertising

The free version of Funpath displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google AdMob may collect and process data such as a device Advertising ID, IP address, and device information. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your choices and applicable law.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we present a consent message (using Google's User Messaging Platform) that lets you choose how your data is used for ads. You can change your choice at any time from the in-game settings ("Privacy options"), where available.

Purchasing the one-time Full Unlock permanently removes advertisements from the game.

For more information about how Google uses data for advertising, see https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how players interact with the game (for example, which levels are completed and which features are used) so we can improve the experience. Firebase Analytics may collect usage events, app/device identifiers, device and OS information, and coarse location derived from IP address. This data is used in aggregate for analytics purposes.

See Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy and Firebase privacy information at https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

In-App Purchases

Funpath offers optional in-app purchases (for example, the Full Unlock and solution credit packs). All purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment card or financial details; this information is handled by Google.

Data Storage and Sharing

Bug report data is stored in Google Forms/Google Sheets and is accessible only to the development team. Advertising and analytics data is processed by Google as described above. We do not sell your information. We do not share your information with third parties except the service providers described in this policy, or as required by law.

Children's Privacy

Funpath is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The game does not require account creation or personal data input. If you believe a child has provided us with information through the bug report feature, please contact us and we will delete it.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob – advertising. https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Google Analytics for Firebase – usage analytics. https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Play Services & Google Play Billing – app distribution and in-app purchases. https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Forms – for receiving voluntary bug reports only.

Data Retention

Bug report data is retained only as long as necessary to resolve the reported issues. Advertising and analytics data is retained according to Google's retention policies. We periodically review and delete old bug report data.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete data relating to you, and to manage your advertising preferences (via the in-game "Privacy options" and your device's Ad settings). If you have submitted a bug report and wish to have that data removed, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
