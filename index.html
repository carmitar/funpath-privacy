Privacy Policy for Funpath

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Funpath ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you choose to submit a bug report through the in-app bug report feature, the following information is collected and sent to us:

  • Bug category and description (as entered by you)
  • Device model and operating system
  • Screen resolution
  • Graphics hardware name
  • Device memory (RAM)
  • Current game level and scene
  • App version

This information is submitted only when you actively choose to send a bug report. It is not collected automatically.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Browsing history
  • Financial or payment information
  • Analytics or usage tracking data

How We Use Information

The information collected through bug reports is used solely to:

  • Identify and fix bugs in the game
  • Improve game performance and compatibility

Data Storage and Sharing

Bug report data is stored in Google Forms/Google Sheets and is accessible only to the development team. We do not sell, trade, or share your information with third parties.

Advertising

Funpath does not contain any advertisements and does not use any advertising SDKs or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The game does not require any account creation or personal data input. The optional bug report feature collects only technical device information and does not request any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services – for app distribution. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Forms – for receiving voluntary bug reports only.

Data Retention

Bug report data is retained only as long as necessary to resolve the reported issues. We periodically review and delete old bug report data.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data automatically, there is generally no personal data to access, modify, or delete. If you have submitted a bug report and wish to have that data removed, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
